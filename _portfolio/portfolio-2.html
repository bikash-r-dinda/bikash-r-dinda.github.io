This portfolio item features a concise research poster titled W-06_Dinda-Bikash Ranjan-Postdoc-Poster.pdf, summarizing key insights from arXiv:2407.17252.

The poster presents a snapshot of model-agnostic reconstruction techniques applied to DESI DR1 BAO data, combined with CMB and supernova observations. It emphasizes the importance of avoiding parametric bias when assessing the evolution of dark energy.

  • Highlights Gaussian Process reconstruction of cosmological distances
  • Shows that DESI’s apparent signal for evolving dark energy weakens under model-agnostic analysis
  • Supports ΛCDM within 1σ across redshift range

This brief visual summary is ideal for quick reference and outreach, especially in postdoc forums and poster sessions.
